What is Save My Resume

Save My Resume is a free resume scoring tool that analyzes your CV instantly in your browser. No account required. No data stored. No paywalls. Everything runs 100% locally on your device.

Upload a PDF or DOCX file, and get a comprehensive report including:

  • Overall score (0-100 scale)
  • 5-category breakdown (Skills, Experience, ATS, Impact, Clarity)
  • ATS compatibility rating
  • Keyword gap analysis
  • Red flag detection
  • Recruiter screening checklist
  • Personalized recommendations
  • Actionable improvement suggestions

Why People Use Save My Resume

75% of resumes are rejected by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) before a human ever sees them. Most job seekers don't know why their resumes fail, and professional resume services are expensive.

Save My Resume solves this by giving you instant, detailed feedback on exactly what needs to change.

Privacy & Security

Your resume is your most sensitive document. That's why we built Save My Resume with privacy as the foundation:

  • Zero Server Communication: Your resume never leaves your device
  • No Data Storage: We don't store, log, or track any of your resume data
  • No Account Required: No login, no email, no personal information needed
  • No Tracking: We don't use analytics, cookies, or tracking technology
  • No Ads: Save My Resume is completely ad-free
  • 100% Rule-Based: Transparent scoring with no black-box AI
